Silhouettes of Empowerment

by Chloe Liu

  • In the mirror,
  • A reflection not just of beauty, but capability,
  • The potential of skills, minds, innovation, often unformed.
  • Yet, in each data point, a revolution unfolds,
  • Where femininity and ability, courageously hold.
  • From mother's womb I took my flight,
  • As a spring of female empowerment,
  • Echoed in the voices of sisters across continents.
  • Waves of female toil are speaking loudly,
  • We are not mere silhouettes of societal imprints,
  • But a creator, a worker, a change-maker.
  • The truths, the stories, the silent pleas,
  • Of women who work, strive, and believe.
  • But a creator, a worker, a change-maker,
  • Fights for equality, hand in hand.
  • Find our reflection, diverse yet aligned,
  • A global sisterhood, empowered and defined.
  • For the fight is not solitary,
  • But rising, resounding, in collective solidarity.
